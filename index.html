What is it about?

What is this website?

This website is to promote the timelapse video I am working on.


Timelapse?

A timelapse is a video made from multiple photos taken with big time interval. The result is a video that seems greatly accelerated which allows to see imperceptible movements such as cloud evolution and nightfall.

Can I have more technical details?

  • The resolution will be 4K. 3840×2160 That is 4 time bigger than full HD, and will look incredible on your next TV.
  • Gear : I use my Pentax K-5 DSLR to take pictures at fixed intervals, using the built-in intervalometer.
  • Maths : For each second of video, I need at least 25 pictures. For 10 seconds, it is 250 pictures. Taking 1 picture every 30s, it takes 2h to take the 250 pictures.
  • Photo : I import and develop the pictures in lightroom. I then export them in JPEG format to video-editing software.
  • Video : The video needs to be stabilized and the flickering corrected.
  • Audio : I then have to find a music that will be a good fit on the video. After that, I edit the video to sync it on the music. Watch the video above and listen carefully to the music. It is synced with the video!
